What We Can Do

Fighting climate change

Get Involved
Take a few minutes to contact your political representatives and the media to tell them you want immediate action on climate change.
Read More...
Energy Efficient
Change light bulbs to compact fluorescents or LEDs. Install a thermostat. Wash clothes in cold or warm (not hot) water.
Read More...
Eat Wisely
Buy organic and locally grown foods. Avoid processed items. Grow some of your own food. And eat low on the food chain.
Read More...
Trim Your Waste
Garbage buried in landfills produces methane. Keep stuff out of landfills by composting kitchen scraps and garden trimmings.
Read More...
Green Your Trip
Transportation causes about 25 per cent of Canada's greenhouse gas emissions, so walk, cycle or take transit whenever you can.
Read More...
Let Polluters Pay
Carbon taxes make polluting activities more expensive, allowing energy-efficient businesses and households to save money.
